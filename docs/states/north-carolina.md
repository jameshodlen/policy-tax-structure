# North Carolina Tax Structure

> **Last updated**: March 2026 | **Data sources**: Census Bureau, BEA, IRS SOI, ITEP, Tax Foundation

## Overview

North Carolina employs a flat individual income tax at 4.25%, a 4.75% state sales tax, and a 2.5% corporate tax rate. The Tax Foundation ranks North Carolina 9th on its State Tax Competitiveness Index.

## Historical Context

- **1921**: Modern income tax enacted with graduated rates
- **1933**: Sales tax enacted
- **2013**: Major reform: graduated income tax replaced with flat 5.8%, corporate rate cuts begin
- **2021**: SB 105: Phased rate reductions from 5.25% toward 3.99% by 2027; corporate phase-out by 2030
- **2025**: Flat rate reaches 4.25%, on track for 3.99% in 2026
