# Kentucky Tax Structure

> **Last updated**: March 2026 | **Data sources**: Census Bureau, BEA, IRS SOI, ITEP, Tax Foundation

## Overview

Kentucky employs a flat individual income tax at 3.5%, a 6.0% state sales tax, and a 5.0% corporate tax rate. The Tax Foundation ranks Kentucky 32nd on its State Tax Competitiveness Index.
