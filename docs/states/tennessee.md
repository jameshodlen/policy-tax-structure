# Tennessee Tax Structure

> **Last updated**: March 2026 | **Data sources**: Census Bureau, BEA, IRS SOI, ITEP, Tax Foundation

## Overview

Tennessee does not levy an individual income tax, a 7.0% state sales tax, and a 6.5% corporate tax rate. ITEP ranks Tennessee's tax system among the 10 most regressive in the nation, with lower-income households paying a higher effective tax rate than wealthier residents. The Tax Foundation ranks Tennessee 11th on its State Tax Competitiveness Index.

### Income Tax

## Historical Context

- **1929**: Hall Tax enacted — tax on interest and dividend income only
- **1947**: Sales tax enacted at 2%, gradually raised to 7% by 2002
- **2016**: Hall Tax phase-out begins, reducing rate from 6% by 1%/year
- **2021**: Hall Tax fully eliminated — Tennessee becomes true no-income-tax state
