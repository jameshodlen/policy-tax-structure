# Washington Tax Structure

> **Last updated**: March 2026 | **Data sources**: Census Bureau, BEA, IRS SOI, ITEP, Tax Foundation

## Overview

Washington does not levy an individual income tax, relying instead on sales taxes, property taxes, and other revenue sources. ITEP ranks Washington's tax system among the 10 most regressive in the nation, with lower-income households paying a higher effective tax rate than wealthier residents. The Tax Foundation ranks Washington 16th on its State Tax Competitiveness Index.
