# New Hampshire Tax Structure

> **Last updated**: March 2026 | **Data sources**: Census Bureau, BEA, IRS SOI, ITEP, Tax Foundation

## Overview

New Hampshire does not levy an individual income tax, relying instead on sales taxes, property taxes, and other revenue sources. The Tax Foundation ranks New Hampshire 6th on its State Tax Competitiveness Index.
