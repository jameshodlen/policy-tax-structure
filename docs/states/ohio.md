# Ohio Tax Structure

> **Last updated**: March 2026 | **Data sources**: Census Bureau, BEA, IRS SOI, ITEP, Tax Foundation

## Overview

Ohio employs a graduated individual income tax with 2 brackets and a top rate of 3.5%, a 5.75% state sales tax. The Tax Foundation ranks Ohio 31st on its State Tax Competitiveness Index.

## Historical Context

- **1935**: Sales tax enacted
- **1972**: Individual income tax enacted
- **2005**: HB 66: Corporate franchise/tangible property tax phased out; Commercial Activity Tax (CAT) phased in; 21% income tax cut
- **2023**: CAT exclusion threshold raised; top income tax rate cut to 3.5%
- **2025**: Rate reduced to 3.5%. CAT threshold raised to $6M, eliminating it for most small businesses.
## Federal Interaction

- **Federal Conformity**: Selective conformity to IRC
