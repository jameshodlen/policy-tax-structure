# Montana Tax Structure

> **Last updated**: March 2026 | **Data sources**: Census Bureau, BEA, IRS SOI, ITEP, Tax Foundation

## Overview

Montana employs a flat individual income tax at 5.9%, and a 6.75% corporate tax rate. The Tax Foundation ranks Montana 5th on its State Tax Competitiveness Index.

## Historical Context

- **1933**: Individual income tax enacted
- **2021**: Structural tax reform — rates reduced, 16 tax credits repealed, corporate apportionment reformed
- **2024**: Income tax simplified to two-bracket system (4.7% and 5.9%)
## Federal Interaction

- **PTET Status**: Adopted — pass-through entity tax available
- **Federal Conformity**: Selective conformity to IRC
