# Analysis

Tax Structure Watch produces deep-dive research on the structural forces reshaping American state taxation. Each analysis page presents primary-source data alongside competing interpretations from center-right and center-left frameworks.

## Published Analysis

### [Who Actually Pays? The Methodology Wars](who-actually-pays.md)

The platform's thesis statement. The Tax Foundation and ITEP are the two most influential organizations analyzing state tax structures. They are both rigorous, both use real data, and they frequently reach opposing conclusions. This analysis explains why — and what it means for evaluating any claim about state tax policy.
